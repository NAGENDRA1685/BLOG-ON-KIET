Welcome to KIET

"KIET Group of Institutions firmly believes in nurturing holistic growth in students, extending beyond the boundaries of the curriculum. As an institute, KIET consistently remains at the forefront, adapting to market requirements and wholeheartedly dedicating itself to the emerging courses it offers."
KIET GROUP OF INSTITUTIONS was founded in 2001 with a clear goal in mind - to provide the best environment for students to develop in all aspects of life. Over the past 25 years, KIET has become a renowned institution within the state and has now established itself as the first AI college in the region. Partnering with IIIT-Hyderabad is a testament to its commitment to excellence. At KIET, we are dedicated to offering quality education and a safe learning environment, ensuring a bright future for our students.

    ⮚ INDUSTRIAL TOURS IN KIET (KIOT) ⇝

    Importance of Industrial Tours

    Industrial tours are an integral part of the curriculum at KIET. They serve several purposes:

  • Exposure to Industry Practices: Students get an opportunity to witness industrial processes and practices, giving them a deeper understanding of theoretical concepts learned in classrooms.
  • Networking: Industrial tours enable students to interact with industry professionals and build valuable connections for future career opportunities.
  • Practical Learning: By observing industrial operations firsthand, students can bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical applications.
